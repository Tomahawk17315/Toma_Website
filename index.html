About

Team Tomahawk, FTC 17315, is a dynamic robotics team from Westwood High School in Austin, Texas. Founded in 2019, we combine technical skill, creativity, and teamwork to excel in FIRST Tech Challenge competitions. Our journey includes remarkable achievements at the regional, state, and world levels—most notably as division champions and world semi-grand finalists at the 2024 Houston World Championship. Committed to STEM outreach, we mentor younger students and inspire our community. Team Tomahawk is proud to represent our school and city, demonstrating what’s possible through dedication, innovation, and the power of collaboration. finalists at the 2024 Houston World Championship.
